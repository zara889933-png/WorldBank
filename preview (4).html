Currency Exchange

The unified digital currency is used to rebuild trade, resource valuation, and labor settlement in underground civilization.

Institutional Role of UEC

UEC is a central digital currency uniformly issued and managed by a global joint financial institution.
It does not rely on paper currency circulation or the credit of any single country, but instead serves the overall coordination of resources and transaction order across underground city society.
The system emphasizes stability, unified settlement, traceability, and coordination across underground cities.

About Us

This website prototype turns institutional ideas from a science-fiction world into a visual and interactive digital platform.

Project Background

The project is based on the underground city society in The Wandering Earth and explores how humanity might rebuild a unified economic order after the old world’s monetary system loses its foundation.
The website prototype shows the naming, issuance, exchange, accounts, payments, network coordination, and governance security of the new currency.

Currency Design Logic

The platform manages UEC circulation through a central issuing authority and combines the task system, resource exchange, and account system to create a closed loop of value creation, distribution, and use. All transactions and exchanges are recorded and coordinated by the system to ensure fairness and efficiency.
As the unified digital currency of underground cities, UEC derives its value not from national credit or market fluctuations, but from survival resources and social contribution. Through a unified pricing system, the platform can maintain a stable exchange order in a resource-constrained environment.

Risk & System Governance

In a resource-limited environment, the system must address transaction risk, unequal resource distribution, and information security issues.
Therefore, the platform introduces review mechanisms, appeal processes, and behavioral monitoring to maintain stable system operation.

Project Significance

This project is not only a website prototype, but also an exploration of how society may be organized in extreme future environments. It asks: when resources become the central constraint, how should humanity redefine value and exchange?
At the same time, why is a unified currency needed? When surface assets lose meaning and national borders dissolve, the original currency system becomes difficult to maintain. As a unified digital currency, UEC enables efficient coordination and resource exchange among different underground cities.
